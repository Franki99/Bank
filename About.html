The DERN Bank is about online banking. Online-only banks may not provide direct ATM
access but will make provisions for customers to use ATMs at other banks and retail
stores. They may reimburse you for some of the ATM fees charged by other financial
institution. Reduced overhead costs associated with not having physical branches
typically allow online banks to offer significant savings on banking fees. They also offer
higher interest rates on savings accounts.
